# Support

The Self Publishing AI Playbook is distributed as a standalone Gen AI University release.

## What Issues Are For

Use GitHub Issues for:

- Installation problems
- Broken links
- Packaging or ZIP problems
- Claude plugin loading problems
- Security concerns that do not require private disclosure

For private security reports, use the guidance in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Canonical Package

Gen AI University maintains the canonical version of this package.

The MIT License allows you to copy, modify, fork, and adapt the project for your own use. The public issue tracker is not a roadmap, consulting queue, or custom publishing support channel.

## Publishing Help

This plugin can help structure the AI-assisted workflow, but it is not a substitute for human judgment, author voice, professional editing, publishing strategy, or accountable coaching.

For deeper publishing support, Gen AI University is an affiliate partner of [Self Publishing School](https://selfpublishingschool.com/). If you enroll in a Self Publishing School program through our referral, Gen AI University may receive a referral commission. We refer authors there when human publishing guidance, coaching, accountability, and support are the right next step.
